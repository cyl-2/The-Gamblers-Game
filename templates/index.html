The Gambler's Game

Why are you here?

You were previously a joker but you decided to change your career so you went to a casino to become a gambler and surprisingly, you're kind of good at it. But some annoying birds broke into the casino and they scared all the other gamblers away. Basically, a bird gang took over the casino. Thankfully for you, when the other gamblers ran away, they left behind some money (because any normal person would value their life over money) so you decided to give their lonely coins a new home (ie. your pocket).
Be careful though...the phoenixes want to ruin your career by killing you and then steal your coins...but you can throw the uno reverse card on them and kill them instead and you can steal their diamonds!

Game Instructions

  1. Collect as many coins as you can in 1 minute.
  2. Avoid or kill the phoenixes because if you don't, you won't see tomorrow's dawn.
  3. Throw your flashing balls at the birds for some diamonds :)

Game controls

Use your keyboard keys to:

  • ↑ Move up
  • ↓ Move down
  • → Move right
  • ← Move left
  • Press "SPACEBAR" to throw balls
  • Hold the "SHIFT" key to move onto tables/chairs/any other object other than the floor.
Please press one key at a time - pressing 2 or more keys at once may result in...abnormalities.

How much is each item worth?

  • Gold coin : +€500
  • Diamond : +€2000
  • Sack of gold : +€1500
If you collect the sack of gold, you will miraculously move faster for 5 seconds because the weight of the gold provides immense motivation ;)

The difference between easy and hard level

Easy level: Birds are super dumb - they only enter the casino through one window and they all fly in the same path.
Hard level: Birds evolved! They're smarter this time and they can enter the casino through any window...they will also CHASE you until you die!
